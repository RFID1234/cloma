About Us

Cloma Laboratories is one of the most trusted brands in the sports nutrition industry today. Each day we work to earn the trust with athletes in every product we make. We strive to deliver the highest quality products in order for every athlete to reach the optimal performance and enable them to compete harder.

Cloma Laboratories ephedra stacks are manufactured in state of the art facilities in the USA that are duly licensed and regularly inspected by FDA and other governmental and independent authorities. The manufacturing facilities are certified by current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP). The facilities are also approved by leading certifying bodies domestically and internationally.

Our Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), implemented by our Quality Control team have been reviewed with FDA representatives.

At each step of the manufacturing process, quality control and quality assurance professionals ensure that all specifications are met. Raw materials are tracked from shipment to receipt to production. Finished products are subjected to additional testing in house and by 3rd party laboratories using the latest technology and equipment to verify the potency and purity. Only after these steps are completed reviewed and approved is the finished product released for shipment.
